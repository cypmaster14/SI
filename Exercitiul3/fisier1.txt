As Aerosmith sang many years ago, “Code looks like a Chain.” No? They didn't say that? Maybe I'm remembering it wrong. Well, as you might have guessed, in this post, we'll talk about the Chain of Responsibility design pattern.
This pattern is not one of the more popular ones, at least among the patterns defined by the Gang of Four,
but modern dependency injection frameworks give us the possibility to implement it in a smart, and fresh way. Let’s see how.
